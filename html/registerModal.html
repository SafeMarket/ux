Instead of using a database of username/passwords, SafeMarket uses a passphrase-encrypted keyfile to manage your account. To get started, you'll need to select a passphrase.

Tips on chosing a strong passphrase

  • Make it long.
  • Use words that aren't in the dictionary, like proper nouns and slang.
  • Use punctuations and numbers.
  • Remember it!
