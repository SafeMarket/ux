Instead of using a centrally owned database of username/passwords, SafeMarket uses a passphrase-encrypted authfile to manage your account. Avoid storing your authfile on cloud services (like GMail or DropBox), and back it up on another device you own (like a thumb drive). If you lose your authfile, you will lose access to your account.

To get started, you'll need to select a passphrase. Tips on chosing a strong passphrase:

  • Make it long.
  • Use words that aren't in the dictionary, like proper nouns and slang.
  • Use punctuations and numbers.
  • Don't forget it!
